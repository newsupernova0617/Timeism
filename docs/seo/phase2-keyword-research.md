- Industry reports on "server time checker" (5K monthly market)

**Note:** Exact volumes require paid SEO tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs). These estimates are conservative and based on public data.

### Difficulty Assessment Methodology

Keyword difficulty rated on:
- **Domain Authority of top 3 results** (high if established brands rank)
- **Content quality of competitors** (low difficulty if guides are basic)
- **Number of competing pages** (low difficulty if <100K results)
- **Niche positioning** (low difficulty if keyword combination is new/niche)
